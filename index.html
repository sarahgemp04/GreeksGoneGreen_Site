Who are we?

Greeks Gone Green is a student-run organization comprised of Greek affiliated students dedicated to enhancing environmental awareness within the Greek community and helping this community to operate in ways that are environmentally responsible.

Our Purpose

Greeks Gone Green is a nonprofit organization dedicated to the three G’s: Greek unity through community involvement; Global, regional and local environmental awareness; and Gateways to sustainability within UCSD. Greeks Gone Green promotes “green” thinking and solutions to not only Greek organizations but also to the greater UCSD community.

Our Mission

"To enhance environmental awareness and promote sustainable practices within the UCSD community that individuals can incorporate into their own lives and extend beyond."

You! Join us!

Students from all backgrounds are welcome and we encourage members to have diverse interests. To become a member of Greeks Gone Green, one must attend a regular weekly meeting and register with the Co-Presidents and Vice President of Administration by providing contact information. Members are identified by a Member Roster maintained by the Vice President of Administration and Co-Presidents.
